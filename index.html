WELCOME TO OFORIKROM MUNICIPALITY

Oforikrom Municipal District is one of the forty-three districts in Ashanti Region, Ghana. It was elevated to municipal district assembly status to become Oforikrom Municipal Assembly. The municipality is located in the central part of Ashanti Region and has Oforikrom as its capital town.
