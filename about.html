About US

Oforikrom Municipal District is one of the forty-three districts in Ashanti Region, Ghana. It was elevated to municipal district assembly status to become Oforikrom Municipal Assembly. The municipality is located in the central part of Ashanti Region and has Oforikrom as its capital town.Oforikrom Municipal Assembly was carved out of the Kumasi Metropolitan Assembly (KMA) and established by LI 2291, being inaugurated on March 15, 2018. It is one of 43 MMDAs in the Ashanti Region, with its capital being Oforikrom. The municipality aims to be a model of decentralized governance, fostering inclusive development and equal opportunities.
